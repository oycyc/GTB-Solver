Guess the Build Word List Solver

What is this?

Guess the Build Solver is a word list cheat for all the themes on the Hypixel gamemode.
By entering the hints given at the 90 second mark,
the solver will return a list of all the possible words for that hint combination.
For more information, click the "Help & Usage" button above.

Simply enter the hints in the textbox, including underscores(_).
Tip: For spaces, do not use an underscore for better results.
